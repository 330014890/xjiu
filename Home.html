    • UTS is a public university of science and technology. We support the economic, social and cultural prosperity of the community. We measure the success of our students, employees and partners and are committed to research, innovation and dissemination of knowledge of public value. We are and will always be an inclusive University.

    Sydney University of science and technology has a multicultural campus life and a vibrant international exchange, learning and research program to prepare graduates for today's and future work. Our campus is located in the center of Sydney's creative and digital District, adjacent to Sydney's central business district. After 10 years of significant development, the continuous transformation of Sydney University of science and technology campus will ensure that we continue to maintain and develop a purposeful and sustainable campus to support innovation in education and research.
